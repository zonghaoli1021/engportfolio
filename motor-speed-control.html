01 // DESIGN CHALLENGE

Move quickly while protecting a dynamically unstable payload.

The Constraint

The course brief required a one-foot 8020 aluminum bar to stand only on its lower face, with no glue, fasteners, or side supports. Abrupt acceleration or braking could therefore cause the load to tip during transport.

The Objective

The project combined simulation and a physical prototype challenge: transport the bar across an assigned distance and back, then bring it to rest without toppling, while pursuing the shortest possible completion time.

02 // REQUIRED ENGINEERING WORKFLOW
